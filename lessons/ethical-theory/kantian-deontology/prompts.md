# Image prompts — Kantian Deontology ("The Cat at the Door")

Ships with hand-authored SVGs in `/assets`. For richer raster (PNG) art, generate
with the prompts below, drop the file in `/assets/` with the matching name, and
swap the `src` in `index.html`.

**House style (every prompt):**
> 16-bit SNES-era pixel art, limited palette, crisp hard edges, dark slate-blue
> background (#1d2235), centered subject, no text, no watermark.

| File | Subject | Prompt addition |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| `cat-at-door.png` | The cat (the "murderer") | A sly grinning cartoon cat (blue-grey, Tom-and-Jerry style) peering through a dim doorway, narrowed green eyes, sharp toothy smile. |
| `mouse.png` | The mouse (you) | A small nervous brown cartoon mouse with big round ears and a sweat drop, wide worried eyes. |
| `kant.png` | Immanuel Kant | 18th-century Prussian philosopher, powdered white wig with side curls, grey coat, white cravat, stern level expression. |
| `korsgaard.png` | Christine Korsgaard | Contemporary woman philosopher, shoulder-length brown/grey hair, round glasses, teal top, warm expression. *(Stylized depiction, not a photoreal likeness.)* |
| `oneill.png` | Onora O'Neill | Older contemporary woman philosopher, short grey hair, glasses, dark navy top, calm level expression. *(Stylized depiction.)* |
| `crown.png` | Kingdom of Ends | An ornate golden crown with jewels, glinting — symbol of the community of rational legislators. |
| `gear-machine.png` | The Universalizer | Two interlocking gears (one green, one magenta), a clockwork "logic machine" look. |

> Note: depictions of living/recent philosophers are stylized educational icons,
> not realistic portraits.
