# Image prompts — Patient Autonomy ("House Calls in the Hedgerow")

Ships with hand-authored SVGs in `/assets`. For richer raster (PNG) art, generate
with the prompts below, drop the file in `/assets/` with the matching name, and
swap the `src` in `index.html`.

**House style (every prompt):**
> 16-bit SNES-era pixel art, limited palette, crisp hard edges, dark slate-blue
> background (#1d2235), centered subject, no text, no watermark.

| File | Subject | Prompt addition |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| `doctor-bag.png` | The doctor's bag | A classic black leather medical bag with a brass clasp, a small green medical cross, country-doctor feel. |
| `peter-rabbit.png` | Peter Rabbit | A brown rabbit in a little blue jacket with brass buttons, tall ears, glancing aside a touch guiltily. |
| `jemima-duck.png` | Jemima Puddle-Duck | A white duck in a blue poke bonnet and a pink-trimmed shawl, naive and trusting. |
| `squirrel.png` | Squirrel Nutkin | A cheeky red squirrel with ear-tufts and a huge bushy tail, wide impulsive eyes, a grin. |
| `hedgehog.png` | Mrs Tiggy-Winkle | A sensible brown hedgehog washerwoman in a white mob cap and apron, kindly and no-nonsense. |
| `fox.png` | The sandy-whiskered gentleman | A sly orange fox in a tweed jacket with long sandy whiskers and narrowed, scheming eyes. |

> Note: characters are stylized, gently grown-up takes on Beatrix Potter figures;
> the bioethics content (informed consent; Emanuel's four models) is the real lesson.
