# Image prompts — Bioethics: The Four Principles ("Rounds with Van Helsing")

Ships with hand-authored SVGs in `/assets`. For richer raster (PNG) art, generate
with the prompts below, drop the file in `/assets/` with the matching name, and
swap the `src` in `index.html`.

**House style (every prompt):**
> 16-bit SNES-era pixel art, limited palette, crisp hard edges, dark slate-blue
> background (#1d2235), centered subject, no text, no watermark.

| File | Subject | Prompt addition |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| `van-helsing.png` | Doctor Van Helsing | An older bearded physician, grey hair and beard, small round glasses, dark green coat, white collar, wise and kindly. |
| `vampire.png` | The Count | A pale aristocratic vampire, slicked black hair with a widow's peak, glowing red eyes, small fangs, high-collared black cape with crimson lining. |
